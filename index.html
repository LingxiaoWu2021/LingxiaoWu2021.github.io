Biography

Dr. Lingxiao Wu (Leo) is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at HEC Montreal, Canada. He received his PhD degree in Transportation Studies from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2020, supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme. He obtained his MSc degree in Management Science from Tongji University, China and his BSc degree in Logistics Management from Dalian Maritime University, China.

His research expertise focuses on integrating operations research and data analytics to solve complex logistics and transportation problems, particularly in aviation and maritime systems. His work has appeared in leading journals including Operations Research, Transportation Research Part A/B/C/D/E, and Transportation Science.
