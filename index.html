Biography

Dr. Lingxiao Wu (Leo) is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at HEC Montreal, Canada.

His research integrates operations research and data analytics to solve complex logistics and transportation problems in aviation and maritime systems.

Research Interests

  • Aviation Operations Modelling
  • UAV Service Network Design
  • Shipping and Port Operations
